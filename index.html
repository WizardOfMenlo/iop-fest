About the day

Focus

IOPFest celebrates experimental thinking, practical cryptanalysis, and open exchange. We keep it lightweight, curious, and hands-on.

Format

Short talks, lightning demos, and roomy breaks for coffee and conversation. Bring questions, not slides.

Audience

Researchers, engineers, students, and anyone fascinated by the craft. No prior registration fee, just RSVP.
